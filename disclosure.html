Affiliate Disclosure

AeroInfill participates in affiliate programmes. This page explains exactly how those relationships work, what they mean for our recommendations, and what your rights are as a reader.

Plain English Summary

The Short Version

◆ Disclosure Statement

AeroInfill earns a commission when you click certain links on this site and make a purchase — primarily through the Amazon Associates programme. This commission comes at no additional cost to you. The price you pay is identical whether you use our link or navigate to the product directly. Our editorial rankings and verdicts are not influenced by these commissions.

That is the summary. The rest of this page provides the full legal detail and explains precisely how our affiliate relationships work in practice.

Amazon Associates

Amazon Programme

AeroInfill is a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Programme, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to amazon.ca, Amazon.co.uk, Amazon.ca, and other Amazon regional stores.

When you click a product link on AeroInfill that points to Amazon, a tracking cookie is set in your browser. If you make a purchase within the Amazon cookie window (typically 24 hours for direct purchases; 90 days for items added to cart), AeroInfill receives a commission on that purchase.

Key points:

  • The commission is paid by Amazon — your purchase price is not affected
  • The commission applies to items added to your cart in the same session, not only the specific product linked
  • Cookie windows vary; the standard Amazon cookie window is 24 hours from click
  • Commission rates vary by product category — typically 1–8% depending on the category

Amazon affiliate links are marked with our associate tag in the URL. You can identify them by the tag=aeroinfill-20 parameter in the URL. All affiliate links on AeroInfill open in a new tab and include the rel="nofollow noopener sponsored" attribute.

Editorial Independence

How This Affects Our Reviews

The question every reader should ask: does affiliate revenue affect which products are recommended? The honest answer is: it can, at sites that allow it to. At AeroInfill, it does not, by the following policies:

  • Rankings are based on testing performance, not commission rates. Amazon commission rates vary by product category, and we do not adjust rankings to favour higher-commission products. A $30 filament spool at 3% commission is ranked based on our filament test results, not below a $1,200 printer at 2.5% commission because the printer pays a higher dollar-value commission.
  • Products without affiliate links are reviewed and ranked equally. Some products we review do not have affiliate links available — they may not be on Amazon, or may not have an affiliate programme. These products are ranked purely on performance, and their absence of affiliate links is not held against them.
  • Negative verdicts are published. Products that performed poorly in our testing receive honest negative assessments even when we carry affiliate links for those products.
  • We do not accept payment for coverage or positive ranking. Manufacturers cannot purchase editorial coverage, sponsored rankings, or guaranteed positive reviews on AeroInfill.
  • Review samples do not guarantee coverage. Manufacturers may provide products for testing. We are under no obligation to publish a review or to publish positively. Test samples are disclosed in individual reviews where applicable.

Legal Compliance

FTC & Legal Requirements

In accordance with the United States Federal Trade Commission's guidelines on endorsements and testimonials (16 CFR Part 255), AeroInfill discloses material connections between our site and any products or services reviewed or recommended on this site. Affiliate commissions constitute a material connection and are disclosed on each article page where affiliate links appear.

We comply with applicable disclosure requirements in the United States, Canada, and the United Kingdom. If you are accessing AeroInfill from another jurisdiction with specific disclosure requirements, please note that our disclosures are designed to be comprehensive and transparent regardless of jurisdiction.

This disclosure was last updated January 2025. If you have questions about our affiliate relationships, contact us at the address on our contact page.
