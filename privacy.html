Privacy Policy

How AeroInfill collects, uses, and protects information when you visit this site.

// Last updated: April 2026

Overview

The Short Version

AeroInfill does not sell your data. We do not run ads. We do not require account registration. The only personal information we collect is what you voluntarily submit through our contact form, and what standard web analytics tools record automatically (page views, referral source, country — no personally identifiable information).

Information We Collect

What We Collect

Contact form submissions: When you use our contact form, your name, email address, and message are transmitted to Formspree (our form processing service) and forwarded to our email inbox. We use this information solely to respond to your enquiry. We do not add you to any mailing list without your explicit consent.

Analytics: We use basic, privacy-respecting analytics to understand which content is useful to our readers. This may include page views, approximate geographic location (country level), browser type, and referral source. No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics.

Cookies: We do not set any first-party cookies. Third-party services (such as Amazon, Google Fonts, and Formspree) may set their own cookies in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Third Parties

Third-Party Services

AeroInfill uses the following third-party services, each of which operates under its own privacy policy:

  • Amazon Associates (CA & US): We participate in the Amazon Associates Programme. When you click an affiliate link, Amazon may set cookies to track your purchase for commission purposes. See Amazon's Privacy Notice.
  • Google Fonts: Fonts are loaded from Google's servers. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Formspree: Contact form submissions are processed by Formspree. See Formspree's Privacy Policy.
  • GitHub Pages: This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. See GitHub's Privacy Statement.
